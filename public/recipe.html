Cinnamon Rolls Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • three-fourth cup warm milk
  • 2 and one-fourth teaspoons of yeast
  • one-fourth cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 whole egg + 1 egg yolk
  • one-fourth cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • two-thirds cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 and a half tablespoons ground Cinnamon
  • one-fourth cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After the dough had doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll.
  9. Cut the rolls into 3 cm sections and place flat into a baking tray
  10. Preheat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rolls for 20-25 mins until lightly brown.
